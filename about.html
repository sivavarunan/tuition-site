how my classes work

Every student arrives at a different starting point. The flow below is what most
classes follow — but the pace and emphasis are tailored to each student.

Understand

First chat to find out the syllabus, the teacher's style, what's already clicking, and where the gaps are. Free of charge.

Plan

A simple study plan with weekly goals, term-test checkpoints, and a target O/L paper score. Shared with parents on request.

Teach

Classes built around examples and questions, not slides. We work problems together until the method is yours, not mine.

Practice

Short weekly homework, past-paper drills, and mistake reviews. Doubt-clearing on WhatsApp between classes.

Track

Regular progress check-ins so the student, the parent, and I are all on the same page about what's improving and what still needs work.

Deliver

Confidence on exam day. Whether that's a Grade 7 term test or an O/L paper, you walk in knowing what you know.
